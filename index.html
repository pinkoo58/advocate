Section 138 is a serious criminal matter. You must respond to the legal notice within 15 days of receipt. Ignoring it can lead to an arrest warrant. We recommend scheduling an immediate consultation to draft a strategic reply and prepare your defense for the trial at the Ranikhet or Almora courts.
